Who are we?

Global Movers is an automotive dealership with it's Headquarters in Eldoret, Kenya, but with a global presence. We have a vision to be the global leader in all things automotive. Our global network allows us to deal in everything from new and used vehicles to vehicle spare parts. We also offer easy access to vehicle financing, through our partners in the financial sector. Contact us today for all your vehicular needs, no matter where in the world you are.

Growing up in a third world country, I always heard how difficult it was to own any vehicle past a bicycle. While the economic situation definitely played a part, there is no denying the role of the government and middle-men in aggravating the situation. Goverment imposed exorbitant taxes on vehicles while middle-men demanded their pound of flesh. While I cannot to anything about government taxes, I can definitely cut-off explotative business-people. Join me in this journey.
